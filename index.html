Welcome to the wasteland, Courier.

To the town of Agua Fria rode a stranger one fine day...

Despite being over 14 years old at this point, Fallout: New Vegas remains one of the best regarded entries in the series. This blog is here to help you get started by introducing you to the wonderful world of the Mojave Wasteland. Filled with cowboys, mutants, and a struggle for the fate of New Vegas and its inhabitants, it is certainly a story worth experiencing for yourself. After all, in what other post-apocalyptic game can you gain the respect of a gang of Elvis Presley impersonators by giving a cyborg dog a brain transplant in a ski lodge full of mutants?
